# Human Agency

## Stewardship Principle — Layer 1

Human Agency is a Stewardship Principle. It governs who holds final decision authority in any relationship action within the Cuqou ecosystem.

---

## Canonical Question

> "Who has the right to make the final decision about this relationship action?"

---

## One Sentence

**EN:** Human Agency is the principle that the human — not AI — holds final decision authority over every relationship action: what to send, whether to send, and to whom.

**TH:** Human Agency คือหลักการที่ว่ามนุษย์ — ไม่ใช่ AI — เป็นผู้มีอำนาจตัดสินใจขั้นสุดท้ายในทุกการกระทำที่เกี่ยวกับความสัมพันธ์: จะส่งอะไร ส่งหรือไม่ส่ง และส่งถึงใคร

---

## Definition

Human Agency is the structural guarantee that no relationship action within the Cuqou ecosystem proceeds without explicit human choice.

It answers not "what was sent?" but "who decided to send it?"

The system may assist in preparing, suggesting, and refining. The human retains the authority to proceed, modify, defer, or cancel — at every stage.

---

## What AI May and Must Not Do

| AI may... | AI must not... |
|---|---|
| Suggest the right moment to reach out | Decide to send without human choice |
| Draft message options for review | Override the human's selected option |
| Recommend which protocol fits | Choose the protocol on the human's behalf |
| Offer to refine, translate, or reorganize | Commit to an action the human hasn't confirmed |
| Coach the human through preparation | Send any communication autonomously |
| Simulate how a conversation might go | Replace the human's judgment with its own |

---

## The Four Cases

Human Agency is best understood through its combinations with [Human Contribution](human-contribution.md):

| Situation | Agency | Contribution | Outcome |
|---|---|---|---|
| AI writes everything; human presses Send | ✅ High | ❌ Low | Agency intact, but voice is AI's |
| AI offers 5 drafts; human rewrites until it feels right | ✅ High | ✅ High | This is Cuqou's goal |
| AI sends message autonomously on schedule | ❌ None | ❌ None | Violates both Agency and Contribution |
| AI fixes spelling/grammar; content is human's | ✅ High | ✅ High | Full assistance, no replacement |

**The goal is not to limit AI capability. The goal is to ensure human authority remains at the center of every relationship action.**

---

## How Agency Differs from Contribution

Human Agency and Human Contribution are distinct principles that can diverge in practice:

- **Agency** asks: *who decided?* — the authority question
- **Contribution** asks: *whose voice is present?* — the authenticity question

A person can have high Agency (they chose to send) and low Contribution (the words are almost entirely AI's). Cuqou cares about both, because relationships are sustained by genuine human presence — not just human permission.

---

## The System Responsibility

The system must:
- Preserve the human's ability to review any action before it occurs
- Preserve the human's ability to modify any AI-generated content
- Preserve the human's ability to cancel at any stage
- Never make an irreversible relationship action the default path

The system may:
- Make AI assistance readily available and easy to accept
- Suggest that a suggested action may be worth taking
- Default to an AI-assisted draft as a starting point (with modification always available)

## Relationship to Other Principles

```
Human Agency     — the authority layer (who decides?)
        │
Human Contribution — the authenticity layer (whose voice?)
        │
Intent Provenance  — the origin layer (where did the intent begin?)
```

These three principles work together. A relationship expression within Cuqou has full integrity when:
1. A human decided to reach out (**Intent Provenance**)
2. A human was the final authority on whether and what to send (**Human Agency**)
3. The human's own voice is present in the expression (**Human Contribution**)
